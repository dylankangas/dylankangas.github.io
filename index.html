Dylan Kangas attended Michigan Technological University to pursue a bachelor’s degree in electrical engineering in 2019. His undergraduate research primarily focused on training large language models for tasks in natural language processing. He was accepted into the accelerated master’s program in 2023 where his thesis work involved developing robust autonomous surface vessels for the US Army and Navy.
